Shertpeli aspaptar

In the spiritual and cultural history of our people who lived a nomadic life, among the traditional sound instruments, stringed and click instruments occupy a prominent place. They have developed together with a variety of household chores, and have accumulated customs and traditions along the way. Ethnographic instruments such as cherter, yetigen, adirna, keruish were added to the dombra, which used to be considered the only stringed and clicked instrument. Legends and folk beliefs connected with them were formed together.

Dombyra

Dombyra is the most common two-stringed, multi-keyed musical instrument of the Kazakh people. It is an instrument with its own musical character, which occupies an important place in the life of Kazakhs. The dombra, which was first used in the framework of the epic tradition to accompany songs, tolgau, and terme, later contributed to the formation of the genre of instrumental works - tunes.

Zhetygen

Zhetygen is a multi-stringed percussion instrument of the Kazakh people. The general shape is oblong, box-like. A cover made of a thin board is closed on the surface, and sounding grooves are made.
