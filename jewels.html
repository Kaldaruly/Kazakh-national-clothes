Jewels

Jewelry, unlike other kinds of the Kazakh arts, which were housecraft, was of professional character, predetermined by specificity of manufacture. The Kazakh jewelers-zergers (“zerzar” in Persian – “gold”) worked individually, their skill was inherited.

Bilezik

Among the women's jewelry, the most ancient and widely used beauty product is the bracelet. The bracelet can be worn both on one hand and on both hands. It can be made from several parts, cast iron whole and assembled, and there are also open and closed types. That's why they are called som bracelets, jumyr bracelets, karta bracelets, cover bracelets, five bracelets, watch bracelets. Among them, there are also several ring bracelets connected by delicate gold and silver chains.

Syrga

One of the beauty products that girls love to wear is earrings. Kazakh jewelers have mastered the ancient secrets of making earrings. For example, a gold shekel and an earring with a stone were found in the Kargaly archeological excavation, dating back to the period before our era. There are many types of earrings made by Kazakh jewelers, such as moon earrings, stone-eyed earrings, dome earrings, kozaly earrings, keychain earrings, sausage earrings, stem earrings, striped earrings, etc. in different regions.

Sholpi

The next type of jewelry is hair accessories called shashtangga, sholp, shashbau, which girls wear on the ends of their hair. Hairpins and hairpins usually consist of nine tenge attached to elegantly coiled silver cords. Nine is one of the sacred numbers in the Kazakh concept. They continue with each other from length to length and interweave to the ends of the hair. The ornaments hanging from the ends of the hair add a tone to the girl's walk and emit a beautiful melody.
