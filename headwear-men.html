From time immemorial, the Kazakhs have had a very special attitude to headdresses. Pulling off a hat from a person's head was considered an insult, and carelessly throwing one's hat somewhere was tantamount to renouncing personal happiness and good luck...

Borik

Borik or Boryk is a traditional Kazakh headdress; male and female; winter and summer. It usually has a soft tulle, insulated with a strip of fur. Borik - "the oldest Turkic headdress"

Takiya

A takiya is a light men's headdress. There are many types of takiya: high-top, flat-top and pointed-top takiya. Men's takiya do not contain tenge, beads, or beads.

Tymak

Tymak is a winter hat for men. It is sewn from the skin of animals and cattle. Tymak is a winter hat with a high roof, a forehead, two ears, a back skirt covering the nape of the neck, and made of animal.
